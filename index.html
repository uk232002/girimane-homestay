'Switzerland of Karnataka'

Sakleshpur is known for its coffee,
and cardamom plantations.It is about three hours
from the Mangalore airport and boasts of spectacular sceneries.

Manjarabad Fort

Manjarabad Fort stands as a testament to the vision and ingenuity of Tipu Sultan, offering visitors a glimpse into India's storied past. From its strategic significance to its architectural marvels, this historic fortress continues to enthrall and inspire all who venture within its walls.

bisile ghat view point

Bisle Ghat Viewpoint in Karnataka is a stunning natural spot known for its breathtaking views and lush greenery. Scenic Beauty – The viewpoint offers panoramic views of the Western Ghats, showcasing dense forests, rolling hills, and deep valleys.

Manjehalli Waterfalls

Manjehalli Waterfalls, also known as Abbi Falls, is a scenic natural retreat celebrated for its cascading waters and refreshing bathing spots. Nestled amid lush greenery, the falls create a natural pool that offers an inviting escape for visitors looking to cool off and unwind.

Bisle Reserve Forest

Bisle Reserve Forest is a lush expanse nestled in the Western Ghats .A recognized global biodiversity hotspot. The forest is celebrated for its rich mix of habitats, from tropical evergreen to semi-evergreen woodlands, which provide refuge to a diverse array of flora and fauna.

Raxidi Estate

Raxidi Estate is a small village in Sakleshpur which gives the visitor a glimpse into the village life there. One also finds here beautiful greenery and coffee plantations. There are also spices and cardamom plantations here. This makes for an amazing place for nature lovers to visit. The climate here is also quite pleasant.
